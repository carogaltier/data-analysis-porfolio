Mortality data in Argentina in 2019

Highlights

In 2019 in Argentina total amount of deaths was 337.4 K over a total population of 44,94 M.

Disaggregated by sex, the total amount of male deaths is slightly higher (2.2%) than the number of female deaths.

In general, the younger the age group, the lower the amount of deaths (except when the youngest group is considered, due to relatively high mortality rates at early ages).

From all the group of causes, 'Cardiovascular Diseases' is the one responsible of the highest amount of deaths.
